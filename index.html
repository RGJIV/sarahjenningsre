A Partner You Can Trust

A native of Germantown, Maryland, I graduated from NC State University with a degree in Marketing and Communications. After a successful career in high-end events and marketing in Washington, DC, my family and I moved to Cashiers to raise our two boys amid the mountains we love.

During the pandemic I transitioned from marketing at Lonesome Valley (my husband’s family development) into real estate sales. Today I am proud to be a founding Partner at Cashiers Valley Real Estate, where I bring the same dedication and attention to detail that defined my previous career to every client I serve.

Beyond real estate, I serve on several local boards and am deeply committed to our community — because the place we call home matters as much as the home itself.
