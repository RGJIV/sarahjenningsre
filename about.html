About Sarah Jennings

Partner & Co-Founder, Cashiers Valley Real Estate

A native of Germantown, Maryland, I graduated from NC State University with a degree in Marketing and Communications in 2002. My early career was spent in Washington, DC, where I managed high-end corporate events and catering for Design Cuisine and the Smithsonian museums. I later founded my own successful marketing consulting firm before my family and I moved to Cashiers to raise our two teenage boys.

Upon arriving in the mountains, I handled marketing and events for Lonesome Valley (my husband’s family development). During the pandemic I transitioned into real estate sales, and in 2021 I proudly became a founding Partner of Cashiers Valley Real Estate.

Today I bring the same dedication, attention to detail, and personal touch that defined my previous career to every client I serve. Whether you are buying or selling a luxury mountain home in Cashiers, Highlands, or the Southern Blue Ridge, my goal is to make the process seamless and enjoyable.
